About

I'am Fraol

A software engineer with a tremendous amount of enthusiasm to solve problems especially using technology, I have a passion for startups, so I enjoy spending time reading business books and watching videos that help me understand the area. I studied my bachelor's degree at Addis Ababa Science and Technology University, Ethiopia. And after my graduation, I got a chance to join a startup incubation to work on a precision agriculture technology that aims to solve the existing problem within Ethiopian smallholder farmers. Unfortunately, the startup I started didn't go as far as I expected it due to numerous reasons. That is when I started thinking of studying abroad for quality education and also a better startup ecosystem that helps me nurture my desire. After the failure of my startup, I started working as a full-time software engineer in Ethiopia while applying for a Ph.d. programs abroad. Even though it was tough to land a free graduate degree in the USA, I was able to succeed through my persistence. Currently, I'm doing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of North Dakota with a focus on working at the intersection between deep learning and medical imaging.
